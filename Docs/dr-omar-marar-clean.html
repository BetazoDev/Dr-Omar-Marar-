About Dr. Marar

A Surgeon Defined by Training, Teaching & Leadership

Dr. Omar Marar is a board-certified Colon and Rectal Surgeon practicing at Valley Surgical Clinics in Phoenix, Arizona. His clinical expertise is grounded in years of rigorous training at nationally recognized institutions, where he developed a commitment to both surgical precision and compassionate patient care.

Dr. Marar completed his fellowship in Colon and Rectal Surgery at Thomas Jefferson University Hospital, one of the nation's leading academic medical centers. He completed his general surgery residency at St. Barnabas Medical Center, where he built a strong foundation in complex surgical cases.

Dr. Marar's academic work has taken him across leading institutions. He previously held a faculty appointment at Central Michigan University Health, where he shaped the next generation of surgeons. He now brings that same commitment to education and mentorship to his current practice at Valley Multi Specialty in Phoenix.

Clinical Expertise

Specialized Surgical Care

Colorectal Cancer Care

Comprehensive evaluation, surgical resection, and multidisciplinary management of colon and rectal cancers with a focus on oncologic outcomes and quality of life.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Laparoscopic and robotic-assisted procedures for a wide range of colorectal conditions, offering patients shorter recovery times and reduced postoperative discomfort.

Colon & Rectal Surgery

Expert surgical management of benign and malignant conditions of the colon and rectum, including diverticular disease, inflammatory bowel disease, and rectal prolapse.

Digestive Health

Diagnosis and surgical treatment of complex digestive conditions, coordinating care with gastroenterologists to provide integrated, patient-centered treatment plans.

Advanced Surgical Procedures

Complex pelvic floor reconstruction, sphincter-preserving surgery, transanal procedures, and advanced surgical techniques for high-acuity colorectal conditions.

Anorectal Conditions

Specialized treatment for hemorrhoids, fistulas, fissures, and perianal disease using both surgical and office-based procedures tailored to each patient's needs.
